# A simple attendance tracker for college

## Steps to install:

1. Go to [myttendance](https://myttendance.netlify.app)
2. Click on the browser menu button (three dots).
3. Click "Add to homescreen" then click "Install".

## Features:

1. Subject wise attendance
2. Allows multiple classes of the same subject on a day
3. Everything on your device. No ads, tracking or data collection.
4. Works offline
